EOSC welcomes the participation of resource providers that will contribute to develop EOSC into a rich environment offering a wide range of resources for researchers.

EOSC is intended to set off the ground by federating existing scientific data infrastructures and digital infrastructures for data exploitation that are now spread across disciplines and EU member states. This will make access to scientific data and other scientific outputs easier and more efficient.

Why join as a provider?

By contributing your resources to the EOSC portal, you can:

  • Choose to advertise them on the EOSC Portal and promote their adoption outside your traditional user groups, reaching a wider user base.
  • Get statistics about access requests and customer feedback
  • Get a free online platform where you can manage resource requests, interact with users and provide support to them, and agree the most suitable resource levels.
  • Allow users to authenticate with their own credentials to access your resources and get support to enable this.
  • Contribute to the definition and maintenance of EOSC resource provisioning policies and the portfolio roadmap.
  • Join the group of providers that meet EOSC quality standards.

Resource requirements

Resources of the EOSC Portal are provided and maintained by different providers under a variety of licenses. To become a provider we require that:

  • The resource is accessible by users outside its original community.
  • The resource is described through a common template focused on value proposition and functional capabilities.
  • At least one resource instance is running in a production environment available to the user community.
  • Publish Research data is Findable, Accessible, Interoperable and Reusable [reference to FAIR].
  • Release notes and sufficient documentation are available.
  • Helpdesk channels are available for support, bug reporting and requirements gathering.
